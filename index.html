Why Join Us?

Grow your skills

Use and learn the latest tech with great team members and a solid development process that includes things like code review on pull request, CI, automated checks.

Build award-winning apps

Life is too short for crappy apps. We focus on usability, design and code quality. Check out our apps like Spentable and Jamn, which was featured on the App Store with millions of downloads.

Contribute to open source projects

We believe in open source, transparency and tech for social good. You can check out our flagship product Skygear.io, an open source serverless platform for web and mobile apps.

Work with international companies and startups

Our products and services are used by companies from international brands to startups, like Thomson Reuters, Artstack.com, and ASOS. Check out our website for more.

Build products for the dev community

In addition to working on cool client projects, we are developers who make products for other developers.

Work Environment

Feel at home

Think of a cafe and community of developers. That's what our office is. Exchange ideas and the latest gadget reviews with your fellow team members in an open office. Want to work on a sofa? We have that. Want a quiet work space? We have that. Want a yoga mat? We have that too.

Be your whole self

Love coffee, whisky, cats, anime, something else? Bring your hobbies to work. Have suggestions for the company? We're all ears - figuratively on Slack and Quip and literally in the office.

Manage yourself

Have flexible hours. Work from our Taipei office. Work from home if you're feeling under the weather. This is the 21st Century.

Take ownership

Work with people who care about their craft. Collaborate using best practices and people who care about details down to Github commit protocols.
